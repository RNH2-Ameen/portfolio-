PUBLISHED · ELSEVIER 2026 Microprocessors & Microsystems ScienceDirect · Scopus Q2

A High-Throughput Pipelined FPGA & ASIC Architecture for Real-Time Multimodal ECG–PCG Cardiac Classification

Microprocessors and Microsystems, Vol. 122 (2026), Article 105283 · DOI: 10.1016/j.micpro.2026.105283

Introduces mlCardioGuard, a custom dual-stream ASIC eliminating the Von Neumann memory bottleneck via shift-register buffering and ROM-mapped INT8 weights. Achieves a 5-cycle (50 ns) deterministic latency, 2.17 mW total power consumption, and a 35,803.64 µm² silicon footprint on 90 nm CMOS, validated on Xilinx Kintex-7 FPGA + Cadence Genus synthesis.
